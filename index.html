Who is this for?

People working in and around engineering organizations, technical communities, open source, developer experience, and leadership roles — especially those navigating complex socio-technical systems.

What kind of writing is this?

Essays, frameworks, field notes, and pattern analysis. The goal is useful clarity, not motivational fluff or trend-chasing.

Will it only be about AI?

No. AI is one thread, but the broader focus is how people, tools, structures, and incentives interact inside organizations and communities.

Why subscribe instead of just following online?

Because the newsletter is where the ideas get developed with more depth, continuity, and care. Social fragments are not the same thing.
