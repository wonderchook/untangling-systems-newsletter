Untangling Systems

Five landing page directions

Different moods, same newsletter. Each option tweaks layout, tone, and emphasis while keeping your core themes: engineering leadership, governance and community, and AI developer enablement.

Option 1
Editorial / Soft Magazine
Warm + clear

Editorial

Elegant, readable, and publication-like. Strong typography, generous spacing, and a calm sense of authority.

Best if you want it to feel intelligent and inviting without being too severe or too startup-ish.

Open option 1
Option 2
Systems Diagram / Analytical
Structured + crisp

Systems diagram

Leans into networks, relationships, and underlying structures. More “thinking model” than “magazine.”

Best if you want the site to feel like it belongs to someone who studies how organizations actually work.

Open option 2
Option 3
Minimal / Quiet Authority
Minimal + direct

Quiet authority

Stripped down, clean, and confident. Less decorative energy, more trust and clarity.

Best if you want the writing to do most of the work and avoid visual fuss.

Open option 3
Option 4
Bold / Contemporary Publication
Sharper + louder

Contemporary publication

Bigger contrast, stronger blocks, more visual momentum. Still polished, but more assertive.

Best if you want more personality and more conversion energy without going full weird again.

Open option 4
Option 5
Image-led / Human Systems
Human + visual

Image-led

Uses photography/illustration placeholders to make the page feel more alive and less text-only.

Best if you want warmth, approachability, and room for visual storytelling.
